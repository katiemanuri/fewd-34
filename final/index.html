    Yours, Dreamily, is the debut album by The Arcs, a side-project by Dan Auerbach of The Black Keys. The album originated as an Auerbach solo project but morphed into a collaborative effort, with musicians Leon Michels, Richard Swift, Homer Steinweiss, and Nick Movshon. All members of the band share songwriting credit for the album.



    get your drink on



    This crimson-hued cocktail mixes some of our favorite flavors: wine, whiskey and ginger, with a bright splash of lemon. Bloody good.

    • Recipe: New York Ginger Sour
    • 2 ounces (60 milliliters) rye or bourbon whiskey
    • 1 ounce (30 milliliters) Ginger Simple Syrup
    • ¾ ounce (20 milliliters) fresh lemon juice
    • ½ ounce (15 milliliters) dry, full-bodied red wine
